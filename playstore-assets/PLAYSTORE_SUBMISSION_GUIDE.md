## 5. Release Track

### Recommended rollout path:

#### Step 1: Internal Testing (immediate, up to 100 testers)
- Upload AAB to Internal testing track
- Add your own email + 5-10 trusted testers
- No Google review required — instant install via opt-in URL
- Test for 2-3 days

#### Step 2: Closed Testing (optional, 100 testers)
- Broader test group (friends, first students)
- Google reviews the track (1-3 days)
- Can have multiple tracks (e.g. "alpha", "beta")

#### Step 3: Open Testing (optional, anyone who opts in)
- Listed on Play Store as "Early Access"
- Good for gathering feedback before production

#### Step 4: Production
- Public release
- Google review: 1-7 days (usually 2-3 days for first release)
- Staged rollout recommended: start at 5% → 20% → 50% → 100% over a week

## 7. After Upload — First Review Tips

1. **Missing Privacy Policy link:** reject. Make sure `/privacy` is reachable.
2. **Account deletion URL broken:** reject. Test `suttro.app/delete-account` loads publicly.
3. **Target audience < 13:** extra Families Policy review — our target is 13-17, so be explicit.
4. **Google Sign-In requires test account:** provide one in "App access" section.
5. **Screenshots with placeholder text or watermarks:** reject. Use real content.
6. **Icon with alpha/transparency:** Play Store icon must be 32-bit PNG but NOT have transparency in the visible area (our 512 is fine — white bg).

Suttro — Interactive NCTB Class 9-10 Science Simulations for Bangladesh

Suttro is Bangladesh's first interactive science simulation platform, built specifically for NCTB Class 9-10 students. Unlike video-only education apps, Suttro lets you actually interact with physics experiments, chemistry reactions, and biology processes — all fully in Bangla.

Features:
• Interactive simulations in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Higher Math, English
• Daily class archive with chapter-wise recorded lessons
• 840+ MCQ questions and Creative Questions (CQ)
• Complete NCTB-aligned study guide in Bangla
• Daily lessons with spaced repetition
• Google Sign-In for easy access
• Works offline after download

Perfect for: NCTB Class 9 & 10 students, SSC candidates, all departments (Science/Humanities/Commerce).

Free tier includes daily lessons, select simulations, and basic guide. Premium unlocks all simulations, all practice questions, and all video classes.

Keywords: NCTB, SSC, class 9 physics, class 10 science, bangla physics simulation, NCTB physics, Bangladesh education, interactive learning, suttro, সূত্র
